# 栈的挂起与恢复

### 通过命令挂起栈

> ```heat action-suspend <stack_name or stack_id>```

### 通过命令恢复栈

> ```heat action-resume <stack_name or stack_id>```

### 示例如下：

```
# heat action-suspend base_stack
+--------------------------------------+------------+------------------+----------------------+
| id                                   | stack_name | stack_status     | creation_time        |
+--------------------------------------+------------+------------------+----------------------+
| 194f47ee-c28b-4356-a7d3-3f74ce768bbd | base_stack | SUSPEND_COMPLETE | 2015-11-24T08:58:45Z |
+--------------------------------------+------------+------------------+----------------------+
```

```
# heat action-resume base_stack
+--------------------------------------+------------+-----------------+----------------------+
| id                                   | stack_name | stack_status    | creation_time        |
+--------------------------------------+------------+-----------------+----------------------+
| 194f47ee-c28b-4356-a7d3-3f74ce768bbd | base_stack | RESUME_COMPLETE | 2015-11-24T08:58:45Z |
+--------------------------------------+------------+-----------------+----------------------+
```
